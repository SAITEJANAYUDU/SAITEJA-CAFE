Our Story

Established in 2025, SAI TEJA CAFE brings you the perfect blend of traditional Indian flavors and modern cafe culture. Located in the heart of Hyderabad, we pride ourselves on serving freshly brewed coffee and delicious homemade food.

Our cafe is more than just a place to eat - it's a community hub where students, professionals, and families come together to enjoy great food, good coffee, and wonderful conversations.
